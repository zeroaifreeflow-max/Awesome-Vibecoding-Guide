# How to Make Money with Vibecoding

## A Practitioner's Playbook for Websites, Automations & Micro-SaaS

A comprehensive guide drawn from real commercial projects and hundreds of thousands of lines of AI-assisted code. This isn't a tool catalog ‚Äî it's a monetization playbook.

**Core principle:** The LLM behind vibecoding doesn't matter as much as your technique. With proper methodology, even budget models deliver professional results.

## üí∞ Choose Your Revenue Stream

### [1. Local Business Websites](docs/02-websites-playbook/README.md) ‚≠ê Flagship
**Proven model. Quick time-to-cash.**
- Build fast, modern websites for local businesses
- $300-700+ per site, 4-6 hours of work
- Zero hosting costs with Cloudflare Pages
- Recurring revenue through maintenance

### [2. AI Automations](docs/03-automations-playbook/README.md)
**Higher value. Productized services.**
- Automate client workflows (intake, quoting, follow-ups)
- $500-3000 setup + monthly retainers
- Builds on development skills

### [3. Micro-SaaS](docs/04-micro-saas-playbook/README.md)
**Product income. Higher risk, higher reward.**
- Solve one specific pain point
- Validation-first approach
- Subscription revenue
