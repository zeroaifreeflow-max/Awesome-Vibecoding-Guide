# The Money Map

> How to make real money with vibecoding — not hype, but proven approaches.

## Three Revenue Streams

This guide covers three ways to generate income with vibecoding:

### 1. [Local Business Websites](../02-websites-playbook/README.md) ⭐ Flagship
**Best for beginners** — Proven model with quick time-to-cash.
- Build fast, modern websites for local businesses
- $300-700+ per site, 4-6 hours of work
- Zero hosting costs with Cloudflare Pages
- Recurring revenue through maintenance

### 2. [AI Automations](../03-automations-playbook/README.md)
**Best for technical practitioners** — Higher value, productized services.
- Automate client workflows (intake, quoting, follow-ups)
- Setup fee + monthly retainer model
- Builds on development skills

### 3. [Micro-SaaS](../04-micro-saas-playbook/README.md)
**Best for experienced builders with clients** — Upsell tiny tools to existing clients.
- Solve one specific pain point for a client you already have
- Client-first approach (they already trust you)
- Recurring revenue without marketing

## Choosing Your Path

| Stream | Time to First $ | Skill Level | Risk | Recurring? |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|------|------------|
| Websites | 1-2 weeks | Beginner | Low | Optional |
| Automations | 2-4 weeks | Intermediate | Medium | Yes |
| Micro-SaaS | 1-2 weeks | Advanced + Clients | Low-Medium | Yes |
