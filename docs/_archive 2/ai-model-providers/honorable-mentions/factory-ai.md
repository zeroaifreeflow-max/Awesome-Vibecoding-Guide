## Strategic Workflow

Factory AI excels as a cost-effective foundation for hybrid workflows:

1. **Planning Phase**: Use Factory AI's models (or frontier models) to create comprehensive plans and generate `.md` files
2. **Development Phase**: Deploy different LLMs for implementation while maintaining cost control
3. **Iteration Cycle**: Use codex models for rapid prototyping and testing

The $20/month investment becomes a strategic asset for developers who want to leverage multiple models without breaking the bank.
