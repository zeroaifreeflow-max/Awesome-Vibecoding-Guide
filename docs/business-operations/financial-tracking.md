# Pillar 3: Financial Management

## The Vibecoder's Financial Stack

**Essential Tools:**

- **Invoicing**: Wave, FreshBooks, or QuickBooks
- **Expenses**: Expensify or receipt tracking app
- **Banking**: Separate business account
- **Taxes**: Accounting software or CPA
- **Payments**: Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfers
