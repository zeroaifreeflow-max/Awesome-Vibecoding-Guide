### The 2-Hour Rule

**Principle:** Never let any single task take more than 2 hours without a break
**Implementation:**
- Set timer for 2 hours
- Work intensely on one task
- Take 15-minute break
- Reassess priority and approach

**Benefits:**
- Prevents burnout
- Maintains focus
- Forces task breakdown
- Improves estimation

### Time Tracking Tools

**Recommended Tools:**
- **Toggl Track**: Simple time tracking
- **Clockify**: Free unlimited tracking
- **Harvest**: Time tracking + invoicing
- **RescueTime**: Automatic productivity tracking

**Tracking Categories:**
- Development
- Client Communication
- Sales & Marketing
- Admin & Operations
- Learning & Improvement

### Hiring Process

**Step-by-Step Hiring Guide:**

1. **Define the Role**
   - Job title and responsibilities
   - Required skills and experience
   - Time commitment (hours/week)
   - Compensation range

2. **Create Job Description**
   ```
   Job Title: [Position]
   
   About Us:
   [Your business description, mission, values]
   
   About the Role:
   [Brief overview of position and impact]
   
   Responsibilities:
   - [Task 1]
   - [Task 2]
   - [Task 3]
   - [Task 4]
   - [Task 5]
   
   Requirements:
   - [Skill 1]
   - [Skill 2]
   - [Experience level]
   - [Tools/software knowledge]
   
   Nice to Have:
   - [Bonus skill 1]
   - [Bonus skill 2]
   
   Compensation:
   - [$X-$Y]/hour
   - [Z] hours/week
   - [Payment schedule]
   
   How to Apply:
   - Send resume/portfolio to [email]
   - Include [specific request]
   - Deadline: [date]
   ```

3. **Post the Job**
   - Upwork
   - Fiverr Pro
   - LinkedIn
   - Local job boards
   - Your network

4. **Screen Candidates**
   - Review portfolios
   - Check references
   - Initial screening call
   - Technical assessment

5. **Interview Process**
   - 30-minute intro call
   - 60-minute technical interview
   - Paid test project ($50-$100)
   - Reference checks

6. **Onboarding**
   - Sign contract
   - Set up tools and access
   - Provide training
   - Assign first tasks
   - Schedule check-ins

## The Bottom Line

Building strong business operations transforms you from a freelancer to a business owner by:

1. **Creating Leverage**: Systems allow you to do more with less effort
2. **Enabling Scaling**: Processes let you grow beyond your personal capacity
3. **Improving Quality**: Consistency leads to better client results
4. **Reducing Stress**: Organization eliminates chaos and last-minute rushes
5. **Increasing Value**: Professional operations justify higher prices

**Key Takeaways:**
- Start with time management - it's the foundation
- Implement client systems before you need them
- Track finances religiously from day one
- Use simple tools that work for your scale
- Document processes as you create them
- Review and optimize operations monthly
