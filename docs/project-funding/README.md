## Community Support Models

### Why Community Support Matters

Community support enables:
- Free access to core content
- Continued development and updates
- No paywalls for learning
- Inclusive community

### Support Tiers

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Benefits |
|------|---------|--------|----------|
| Supporter | $5 | $50 | Name in sponsors, Discord access |
| Patron | $15 | $150 | All above + early access |
| Sponsor | $50 | $500 | All above + consultation credit |
| Partner | $100 | $1,000 | All above + advisory role |

### What's Funded

Supporters' contributions fund:

| Area | Percentage |
|------|------------|
| Content creation | 50% |
| Infrastructure | 20% |
| Community management | 15% |
| Tool subscriptions | 10% |
| Reserve fund | 5% |

## Sustainable Funding Approach

### Principles

1. **Transparency**: Regular financial reports
2. **Value First**: Always over-deliver
3. **Community First**: No essential content behind paywalls
4. **Sustainability**: Build for the long term
5. **Choice**: Multiple ways to support
