# Digital Products Strategy

> Creating and selling digital products for the vibecoding market.

Digital products offer high margins and scalable revenue. This guide covers products vibecoders can create and how to sell them effectively.

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

| Mistake | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Overcomplicating | Delayed launch | Start simple |
| Underpricing | Less revenue | Research market |
| No testing | Poor reviews | Beta test first |
| Poor support | Bad reviews | Set expectations |
| No marketing | No sales | Plan promotion |

### Best Practices

1. Launch imperfect products
2. Iterate based on feedback
3. Price for value, not time
4. Build email list early
5. Test pricing constantly
6. Automate delivery
7. Plan regular updates
